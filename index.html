About us

Feivel Justice Initiative

is a non-profit, non-governmental, legal and advocacy organization committed to providing legal education, protecting and advancing human rights, access to justice, social justice and ensuring transparency, good governance and accountability in Nigeria.

What we do

Legal Education

We breakdown complex laws, legal concepts and simplify it, using articles and social media I.e Twitter spaces, Instagram Live etc making it readily available to citizens and the general public.

Read More

Social Justice Advocacy

We are very particular about issues ranging from equity, to access to participation. We run campaigns on and off social media e.g the #NigerianDream Campaign in October 2022, inclusion of marginalized communities and individuals,and actively advocating for policy changes.

Read More

Human Rights Advocacy

As a Justice Initiative our focus lies in pushing for inclusion,diversity,equity adherence to the provisions of the Fundamental Human Rights-We have a “Did you Know Series” On Instagram that discusses different rights and their applications.

Read More

Administration of Criminal Justice Reform

We seek to find solutions and support to Victims and individuals in correctional facilities, hence our interest in rehabilitative and restorative justice-Our Story Exchange Program seeks to advance alternatives that give a different meaning to the Criminal Justice system
